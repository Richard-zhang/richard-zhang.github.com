Hack C++ by myself(0)

Before I start, I would like to share some feeling about C++ practice. I used to be enthusiastic in finding “best” C++ book, trying to learn all C++ language by reading. Obviously, it’s useless. Time is consuming and after reading you found out you forget everything.

I prefer study method which is problem-motivated. When I try to solve a specific problem, I will try to think how to do effectively. With the help of search engine dash and books(I prefer to use books as reference instead of learning material), I gradually have a better command of the language by solving problems one by one.

To start my problem-motivated method, I choose hackerank and 计蒜客 as question banks.

This is the first article of Hack C++ by myself series. I hope I can learn one new trick per day as well improving my ability in expanding.

What do I think of me?

This is the first time I try to understand me by answering 9 questions.

1、请你介绍一下你自己，你是个什么样的人？

It’s hard to describe myself. I usually cover my sadness by making me stupid and gain people’s laugh. From people’s ironic face, I have motivation to move on. I don’t like tragedies while tragedies are my nuclear power, helping me to cheer up as soon as I could. I am always afraid the moment tragedies come while at the same time, I am expecting them.
I pay a lot of attention to glories that in my opinion worth to fight.
I am a feminism and deviation to right.
I like a lot of things and easily lost passion. If one thing which I gained and lost passion from for several times, it will finally be my passion.
I have feeling about future but sometimes I am too lazy to make it happen.

2、你有什么理想吗？这个理想是怎么形成的？

When I was a child, I may have lots of profound dreams. However, nowadays, I become what is called Exquisite egoist and hedonist. I never expect I can change the world significantly. Things I can do are to make the social more stable. My dream is to have a peaceful and sunny life after stressful 17 years in my 20s.

3、你理想的伴侣关系是什么样的？你自己在这个伴侣关系中扮演什么样的角色？要承担什么样的责任？

Due to the fact that I am still a teenager, I think my girl friend is the one who motivates you to do better instead of making you comfortable. The most common reason for breaking up is that she decided to be a ordinary people and stop being motivated and encouraging me. Even though I probably end up being a normal people, I still have the dream to do something great. In conclusion, your partner is your cave. Ships can rest in cave but their destinies are to set off. I will do the same for my partner as her cave, encouraging her to achieve her dream independently and let her rest.

4、你理想的事业是什么，你正在做的工作符合你的事业理想吗？这份工作对你的意义是什么？

I am a student. Nowadays, My dream job is to work with smart people and doing something challenging. For example, a trading assistant in Jane Street Capital.

5、你对亲子关系怎么看？对你来说，什么是一个理想的父亲（母亲），你期望自己成为这样一个理想父亲（母亲）吗？

From my perspective, children are angels and devils. Currently I don’t have the ability to face devils.

6、你对钱怎么看，你认为赚到多少钱是足够的？如果你明天一早醒来，已经有足够的钱，你将会如何继续安排自己的生活？

I think money is really important. Money is the necessary condition to have a happy life. While money is not limited in like RMB or dollars, knowledge in some extend is money. Both money and knowledge can make you cost to gain new information lower. Updated and comprehensive information is more important than money. Information can never be fulled hence the day I will have enough money will never come.

7、对你来说，什么是理想的性生活？什么是理想的性道德，在你的性道德观中，什么样的性生活是禁忌的，需要避免的，什么样的性生活是美好的，需要得到鼓励和发展的？

两厢情愿

8、你的择友标准是什么？什么样的人你会愿意交往，什么样的人你会拒绝和他交往？

I don’t like 1. hedonist 2. cynic 3. infantile

9、你对死亡怎么看？你希望自己活到多少岁，你准备怎么度过从现在到死亡的这段时间？如果你要立遗嘱，这份遗嘱会怎么写？

From a TED talk, a scientist predicts that the man that will live forever have been born. I may be the guy.
